For decades, capital of England has been the launch pad for new ideas and trends. It is a creative hub where there is always something new to see and do. Hotels, venues, restaurants, and attractions constantly update and improve. This means no matter how many times you visit, London always has something new in store.


The iconic Eiffel tower is a must in Paris. When visiting in the summer, one can enjoy a lovely picnic on the lawn of the Champs de Mars with the magnificent view of the Eiffel Tower. If the season happens to be winter, there is an ice skating rink on the lower level of the tower.


Manhattan and its neighboring boroughs are home to some of the best restaurants, museums and attractions. But New York is not just a destination. It is also a home. For more than 8 million residents, the city does sleep.
